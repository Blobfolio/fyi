Ask a Yes/No question using the prefix "Confirm", like, "Confirm: Do you like chickens?". FYI will exit with a code of "0" for positive responses, or "1" for negative responses.

USAGE:
    fyi confirm [FLAGS] <MSG>

FLAGS:
    -h, --help         Print this screen.
    -i, --indent       Indent the line.
    -t, --timestamp    Include a timestamp.

ARGS:
    <MSG>    The message!
